Pay a Parking Ticket in Mohali

Overview


If you received a parking ticket in Mohlai City, you can pay it online, by mail, or in person.
To avoid penalties, the City must receive your ticket payment within 30 days of the ticket date.

Guidance

in-person

Can I pay by phone?



No. Our Department of Finance no longer accepts payment for parking tickets by phone.
